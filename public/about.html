Our Story — Where Soil Meets Sun

We began as a small micro-roastery with a simple idea: coffee should be traceable, lovingly roasted, and prepared in a way that honors the farmers. From farm visits to roast profiling, our team obsessively tends to each step.

Founded
2017
Origins
Ethiopia, Colombia, Kenya, Brazil

Direct Trade

We partner directly with farmers, providing premiums and long-term contracts to ensure stability.

Quality Control

Every lot is cupped, profiled, and roasted according to a sensory roadmap so each bag tastes like the best of the origin.

Community

We run farmer education programs and invest in community projects where we source beans.

Transparency Reports

Each bag includes a QR code linking to origin notes, farm stories, and shipment details. Scan to learn the journey from cherry to cup.
